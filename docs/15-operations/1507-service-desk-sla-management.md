# SLA Targets

| Priority | First Response | Resolution Target |
|----------|---------------:|------------------:|
| P1 | ≤ 15 Minutes | ≤ 2 Hours |
| P2 | ≤ 30 Minutes | ≤ 4 Hours |
| P3 | ≤ 4 Hours | ≤ 1 Business Day |
| P4 | ≤ 1 Business Day | ≤ 5 Business Days |
